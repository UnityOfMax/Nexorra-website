PRIVACY POLICY

Nexorra Data Protection & Privacy

Effective Date: March 2026

At Nexorra, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and most importantly, how we protect it. By visiting our website or using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. ZERO DATA SELLING GUARANTEE

Your data is yours. We absolutely do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties or data brokers under any circumstances. We believe in building trust, and that means keeping your information strictly internal and secure.

2. STRICT "NO SPAM" POLICY

We hate spam just as much as you do. When you provide us with your email address or phone number, it is used strictly to communicate with you regarding your specific inquiries, your account, or essential service updates.

  • We will never bombard your inbox with unsolicited marketing emails.
  • We will not subscribe you to mailing lists without your explicit consent.
  • You can opt-out of our communications at any time with a single click or reply.

3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We only collect the absolute minimum amount of information required to provide you with our services. This may include:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, and phone number when you book a demo or reach out to us.
  • Business Information: Basic details about your real estate business to help us tailor our services to your needs.
  • Usage Data: Standard website analytics (like IP address, browser type, and pages visited) to help us improve your experience on our site.

4. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Any information we collect is used strictly to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our appointment generation services.
  • Respond to your questions, inquiries, or support requests.
  • Process transactions and send related information, including confirmations and invoices.
  • Understand how you use our site so we can improve the user experience.

5. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure that any information you provide to us is kept safe and secure against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized Nexorra employees who need it to perform their jobs.

6. COOKIES AND TRACKING

Our website uses basic cookies to ensure the site functions correctly, securely, and to understand how visitors interact with our page. We do not use intrusive, cross-site tracking scripts to follow you around the web.

7. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our website may contain links to other websites (such as our video hosting providers or calendar booking software). Please note that we have no control over the content or privacy practices of those sites, and we encourage you to read their privacy policies if you choose to interact with them.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time as our business evolves. Any changes will be posted directly on this page. However, our core promises—to never sell your data and to never spam you—are permanent and will not change.

9. CONTACT US

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to our team.
