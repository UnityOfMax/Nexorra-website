All services done in-house by our expert team, no outsourcing, no excuses.

01

Appointment Generation

Our system generates leads, nurtures and qualifies them to a referral level standard which means you are only talking to people who genuinly want to buy or sell and have the money and the motivation to move forward.

  • We call, text and email the leads for you
  • No time wasters or tire kickers
  • Booked straight into your calendar fully automatically
02

Done-For-You Qualification

Other agencies generate leads and have you call them but we understand how tedious this can be. We call every lead individually within the first 60 seconds of them entering our system so we can keep the motivation going and make sure they are really ready.

  • Done-for-you calling
  • Automated AI drip campaigns for 2+ years
  • Qualified based on your ideal client
03

Done-For-You System

  • Seamless integration for minimal disruption on your current system

Hi, I'm Patrick. In 2003 I started up my real estate business, learning from my father. I was fortunate enough to be passed on most of his old clients and so I had a great starting point. But after several years, I realized that I couldn't rely on referrals forever if I wanted to build a business that I could scale.

So I began testing different ways to book myself appointments automatically without cold calling or door knocking.

And after more money than I care to admit and a lot of time I built myself a system that got me 60+ appointments every month with real people that I could connect with. And I went from doing 15-20 deals a year to over 40.

After my best year in 2021, closing 58 deals and making close to $700,000 I started getting asked for help by other agents around me.

Using my system I was able to help them double or even triple their business within a year.

So in 2023 I started Nexorra, a company that sets appointments for realtors on demand. In 2024 I shut down my real estate business and went all in on this because I realized how important it was for other realtors.

Now having grown to a team of 7 and helping over 100 agents my goal is to turn this company into an even bigger influence for realtors and to help them even further.

06 Is there a minimum commitment?

We work on a month to month basis which allows you to cancel any time.

07 How does the guarantee work?

We guarantee closed deals within a given time period, if we don't deliver as per our agreement we would refund you everything.
